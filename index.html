About

Moty is a Finance Ph.D. candidate specializing in Blockchain, Modern Payment Systems, and Fintech. With bachelor’s and master’s degrees in software engineering and an MBA in Capital Markets, her interdisciplinary background enriches her research.

Her second line of research is Market Mircostructure. Prior to her doctoral studies, Moty accrued a decade of experience in the stock market. She contributed to international projects like cross-border settlement and market surveillance. Her research offers valuable insights for policymakers in financial innovation.
